## Issues Encountered

### Moondream2 Compatibility Issue

**Problem:** Moondream2 (`vikhyatk/moondream2`) had `trust_remote_code` prompts and `all_tied_weights_keys` AttributeError due to transformers version mismatch.

**Solution:** Switched to BLIP image captioning (`Salesforce/blip-image-captioning-base`) which is more stable and doesn't require `trust_remote_code`.
